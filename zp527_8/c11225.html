A honeymoon is one of life"s most memorable events and just as integral a part of the wedding as the cake and bridal bouquet. Each offers special packages for both the newly love-struck and those looking to save their long-lasting marital bliss. Yet the appeal of the evocative photographs in this book is not just limited to newly-weds. Its glimpses of luxurious suites exclusive venues and compelling recommendations will tempt all its readers to dream and make plans of their own.
