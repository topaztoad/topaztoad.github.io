説明: Ideal for non-PoE Devices: Extend Power for 5 Volt devices up to 328 feet. The GAT-MicroUSB splitter provides 20 watts of continuous 5 volt power to any Micro-USB device. Use with your existing PoE+ Switch or 48v PoE Injector to power devices like Android, Raspberry Pi, Echo Dot, Arlo and more.

Easy set up and installation will allow you to instantly convert 802.3at PoE+ to 5 volts with divided gigabit data. Power will be delivered on the DC output and 10/100/1000 data will be available separately on the RJ45.
