【商品説明】
Zymol Titanium Glaze is Perfect for vehicles that encounter heavy motorway usage or track days. Originally developed for use on SUV"s due to the terrain of the true "Off roader". It is highly resistant to salt, plant sap and proteins; especially effective on factory-applied acrylic paints. Suitable for all paint types and colours. Titanium contains 51% Brazilian No. 1 Yellow and White Carnauba by volume (80% Yellow, 20% White). Directions for using Zymol Titanium Glaze: 1. Put a very small amount of glaze onto the palm of your hand. Use the warmth of your hands, by rubbing them together, to melt Carnauba. With your hands, gently apply the melted glaze in a light, even pattern over no more than a 2 or 3 feet square feet painted surface. 2. Let Titanium stand for 60-90 seconds. 3. Using a moderate to heavy pressure, wipe slowly with a Zymol Microwipe (folded once) to a brilliant lustre before the wax is totally dry. 4. Buff lightly once more approximately 3-4 hour later to perfect the shine. Important: You must use Zymol HD-Cleanse to remove old wax, tar and stains before applying any Zymol Waxes or Glazes; otherwise they will not bond to the paint surface. Ingredients: Carnauba Wax (derived from Palmetto). Montan Oil, Coconut Oil, Banana Oil, Propolis (derived from Bees), Glycerol Separate (derived from Glycerine), Cetyl Esters, Cetyl Cocamide (derived from Coconut Oil) and FD&C Blue #1 Zymol Car Care is now available for sale at Lou Guthry Motors and we are Australian authorized resellers of the Zymol hand crafted system. Zymol products are safe for all paint finishes, including new clear coats, acrylic, urethane and classic lacquers. Zymol Hand Crafted waxes and glazes have a Carnauba content of 37-79% by volume ? 5-10 times the industry standard. Zymol only uses Brazilian No. 1 Yellow and rare No. 1 White Carnauba, the highest grade available.
