　Radiator Guards

This R＆G Radiator Guard is available in black or titanium colour finishes to fit the Suzuki V-Strom 1000 (up to 2013) protecting the radiator guard from damage from road debris．

(For 2014 V Strom 1000 Please see Radiator Guard ＃RAD0173)
R＆G Radiator Guards are now available in a range of new exciting colour options (please note，not all colour options are available for all R＆G Radiator Guard applications)．
Made from pressed aluminium and feature a neat，seamless frame (where applicable)．They improve the looks of your bike and help to protect your expensive and vulnerable radiator from flying debris．
No permanent modifications need to be made to the bike．
