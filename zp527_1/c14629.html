　Chain Guard

This Chain Guard completely replaces the OEM chainguard with complete chain coverage from near the swingarm pivot point to past the rear sprocket．Ensuring the chain is fully protected aswell as preventing material licking up off the chain and onto the tyres．

Made from aluminium，this chainguard is easy to install using existing mounts．Its finished in Brushed Aluminium allowing for a high quality，durable finish that looks fantastic．

Compatible with the Yamaha MT-125’14-．
