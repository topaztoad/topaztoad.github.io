Questions & Answers:
1.Is the time display always on?
NO After two minutes of inactivity the display will darken to save battery. Tap it, time shows again.

2.How to keep showing time?
Make it enter Bluetooth Mode(BLUE) and leave it, after 2 minutes the time will show and NEVER GO OFF.

3.How to switch 12/24H system?
Press forward or backward button to switch, so easy

4.How to set the Alarm?
Press MODE twice, you will see the bell icon. Hold the MODE button, the hour will begin to blink. Adjust with forward and backward buttons until reach the desired hour. Press MODE button to confirm and repeat the same operation to set minute and alarm ON and OFF.

5.Can I use my favourite song for my wake-up alarm sound?
Yes Simply create a folder named as "ALARM" in micro SD card or USB.

6.Can I make one certain color stay?
Yes In color mode, press and hold until the color you like appears then release.
