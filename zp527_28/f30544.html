    • 説明: Polymaker PolySmooth PVB 3D Printer Filament - Polish Your Prints Easily with Alcohol

    PolySmooth is a unique, easy-to-print filament designed for hands-free post-processing. Thanks to our patented Layer-Free Technology, it can be smoothed with alcohol to achieve layer free models using the Polysher. You may have some ceramics feel cool prints after polishing. Our PolySmooth has similar properties and printing settings as PLA, which can be widely used in printing decorations, anime characters, and so on. And the clear filament can be used to print some clearer parts than other materials and for investment casting as well.

    Layer-Free Tech
    Layer-Free technology involves exposing a 3D printed part to an aerosol of micro-sized alcohol droplets, generated by a rapidly vibrating, perforated membrane called the nebulizer. The aerosol will then be absorbed by the surface of the 3D printed part and render it smooth and layer-free.
