Bluetooth Pairing and Connection


1.In the off state, switch on the device. When there is no pairing record, it will automatically enter the pairing mode, and the LED flashes red and blue alternately. The pairing state prompts the sound "Pairing".

3.Turn on the bluetooth of the cell phone and search for new devices. When "M102" appears, click to connect. After the connection is successful, the LED will flash a blue light slowly, and the cell phone displays "M102" connected. You will hear the voice prompt: Your headset is connected.
