    Why Need Support Material in 3D Printing?
    ● If you have a printer with two extruders, you can load one extruder with PLA for printing the model and the other one with a water-soluble material like PVA or limonene soluble material like HIPS for printing the support structure.
    ● Once printing is over, simply wash away the support structure by immersing the model in water or Limonene.
    ● This method of removal reduces the risk of model damage and makes the post-processing work easier. Ideal for complex prints!
    ● Support structures are a necessary evil in 3D printing. You need them to print difficult overhangs and bridges in your model.
