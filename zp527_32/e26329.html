商品コメント Quick Release Monitor Mount: SmallRig quick release monitor mount comprises a quick release plate and a NATO clamp. The quick release plate allows for quick and easy vertical release and setup monitors. NATO clamp on the bottom can be used with standard NATO rails, such as SmallRig 1409/2172, which can realize quick disassembly and assembly, and makes your shooting more efficient and time-saving
360° Swivel & 150° Tilt: The camera monitor holder enables to swivel 360° sideways and tilt 150° with free-stop in any positions and angles you preferred. It enables the cameraman to have better monitoring as per the needs in different shooting scenarios. Besides, its damping of pivot is adjustable through spanner
Tightness Adjustable: The tightness of each axis is adjustable via the Allen wrench. The thumbscrew features holes for hex spanner to ensure your monitor safe. It can effectively prevent twist and don"t need to worry about the monitor fall off. (Note: Please do use the Allen Wrench to re-tighten the thumbscrew and each axis for further secure.)
For 5" or 7" Monitor: SmallRig monitor mount 3601B is designed to hold your monitor onto the top of the camera or other mounting system via NATO clamp. It features 1/4"-20 screw on the top for monitor, light, or microphone, and the quick release plate is reserved for 2 optional dowel pins, which can be removed and installed as needed to effectively prevent the devices from deflection
Durable & Reliable: This quick release monitor holder made of Aluminum alloy, sturdy and durable for a long use. It features a maximum dynamic load capacity of 1.5 kg, supports most monitors and smartphones in the market. And its built-in rubber pads to prevent scratches and increase friction
