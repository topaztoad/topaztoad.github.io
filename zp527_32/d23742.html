商品コメント HAND EMBROIDERED- Each Liliana Cruz dress is hand embroidered and hand sewn in small villages throughout Central and Southern Mexico. The floral pattern on each dress is unique and one of a kind, so variation in pattern will occur. Each dress takes hours and hours of work to create.
VERSATILE- These dresses can be paired with heels for a fancy night out or worn with leggings and sandals for a causal outfit. Can also be worn as a swimsuit cover or great house dress. PLEASE NOTE- As with many white dresses, this dress in white is somewhat sheer and might require a slip or something worn underneath
COMFORTABLE- Cotton blend fabric makes this dress cool and comfortable. Great for hot summer days and warm summer nights. The fabric gets softer with each wash and the embroidery holds up well to machine wash. For longer life we recommend hang dry or dry on gentle cycle.
ETHICAL- Each dress is hand made in women run co-ops operating in small villages throughout central and southern Mexico. The work is distributed throughout the community and allows the women to choose which pattern and style to work on. They then take the patterns to their home to complete and return with the embroidered panel, that is then sewn to the rest of the dress. This type of work provided much needed financial resources, but also allows the women to work on them in their spare time.
SIZING- These are meant to come above knee approximately at mid thigh depending on body type.
